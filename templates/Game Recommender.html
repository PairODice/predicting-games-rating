Game Recommender

Made by Reese and Blake

Here we made a bot that creates graphs based on user inputs. It is a game recommender. You put in a genre, system, and an ESRB rating and out pops three visuals that sucgest games. You can choose what is the most important to you, how critics scored games that fit your requirements, how users rated those games, and how many global Sales those games had. I made this because I have always wanted a game recommender that could help me discover new games. So i took initiative and propoosed the idea. We selected three ideas from our list and this was one of three that won. We took a data set and made it into something that we're proud of. Notice that there may be a different amount of games in the sections because data that is not available.

Below I put "Platform" in the Genre section, "any" in the platform section, which is where you select what console you are on. And in the ESRB rating input I put "E" for everybody. Below, 3 charts are made based on the inputs I put in.

Here in 'Critic Score' is a chart that shows the ratings from critics games that fit the inputs got. These ratings are quite low because critics were pretty harsh. Look at this graph when you test the AI later if you care about how Critics scored the game.

In 'User Score', are the ratings that games recived from users. These ratings are higher than the critic scores because it is people enjoying the game, not judjing it. Look at this graph when you test the AI later if you care about how Users scored the game.

And lastly, the plot 'Global Sales' shows the sales that the games that fit the requirements sold. Look at this graph when you test the AI later if you care about how Global Sales a game has.
